Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the DocScan mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding our practices.

Information We Collect

DocScan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or personally identifiable information, including but not limited to name, address, phone number, or email address.

Any data accessed by the application is used solely for providing the core functionality of the app and is processed locally on your device.

Permissions

The application does not require any permissions of any sorts for the end to end functionality. It does not request any permission at any phase of the operation.

  • Camera access is used as part of the Google ML Kit library for document scanning.
  • The application does not access device files but opens native Document chooser.
  • Files generated by the app (such as PDFs or images) are created and stored only when explicitly initiated by the user. App has no control over it.

At no point does the app access, upload, or share your files externally.

Third-Party Services

DocScan uses Google’s ML Kit library exclusively for document scanning and image processing.

Google ML Kit operates under its own privacy policies, and no user data is shared or transmitted by SimpleScan beyond what is required for on-device processing.

https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms

Data Security

We value your trust and take privacy seriously. DocScan does not collect, transmit or store user data in any way. All processing is performed locally on the device using Android’s ML Kit APIs to generate scanned images or PDF files.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
